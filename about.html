Our Values

Outdoor life is in the DNA of Our Company

Most of our workers at Rainydays have one thing in common: We love to go on short, and long hiking trips.

Before Rainydays came to the marked, we all felt that quality jackets were to expensive. Sure if you bought an expensive top quality jacket, you could probably use it for an expedition to the North pole. But we were not going to the North pole. we just wanted to be able to go out with friends and family in the nearest forest or lake, without having to worry to much about the weather.

There were some low cost jackets too. But we did not trust that they would be good enough, should the weather become really bad.

We established Rainyweather to provide an alternative to expensive jackets. All of Our jackets are enduring 100% water repellent, made for active people in all ages.
